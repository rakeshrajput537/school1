About Us 

                  Global Shine School was established in the year 2018 in the ever developing environment of Phulwarisharif, Patna. The school is recognized for its commitment towards excellence. This is an English medium school.  We at the Global Shine School aim at preparing students to understand, contribute to, and succeed in a rapidly changing society, thus making the world a better and more just place. We pride ourselves on creating a unique, exciting environment that embraces all types of learners and promotes growth both academically and personally. We welcome both students and parents to a dynamic and supportive community.

Our Mission

Believing that education is a powerful agent of social transformation, we will ensure that our students develop the skills that a sound education provides and inculcate in them a sense of compassion, empathy and resilience. We will strive relentlessly - to realize the vision - by making the best use of quality infrastructure, resources and experienced, talented and committed faculty.

           Our Vision             

The vision of Global Shine School is to provide comprehensive educational experiences that will empower our students to become lifelong learners and productive, informed, responsible citizens who are able to meet the challenges of a rapidly changing society. We also aim to bring forth the unique abilities of each child so that they can realise their true potential. We aspire to educate young minds and foster ethical, social and moral values through holistic learning to bloom into responsible global citizens. 

An Educational Atmosphere

Get Admission



Global Shine School welcomes interested applicants for the session 2019-2020. Admissions start from Playgroup to UKG, subject to vacancies. Students are selected through the results of the interview that they have to undergo after applying. Admission is based on the performance of the candidate in the interview. The candidate is graded as per his/her performance and suitability for particular standard. Admission forms for all the classes will be available in the second week of January in the school office.
